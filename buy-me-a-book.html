How the Platform Works

Simple & Transparent: People in need post book requests with their Amazon link and shipping address. Donors browse requests, purchase books on Amazon, and ship directly to the requester.

Your Privacy: Addresses are only visible to donors who want to help. We never store payment information - all purchases happen securely through Amazon.
