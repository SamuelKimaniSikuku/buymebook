🎁 Buy Kindle Books for Others

Welcome, generous soul! Browse Kindle book requests and purchase digital books to gift directly to readers in need.

How to gift a Kindle book on Amazon

  1. Pick a request below and click the "Buy Kindle Book & Gift" button.
  2. A small confirmation shows the recipient's name and email. Keep this tab open — you'll paste the email into Amazon in a moment.
  3. Click OK. A new Amazon tab opens on the exact Kindle edition the requester chose.
  4. On the Amazon page, in the right-hand Buy panel, click "Buy for others" or "Give as a Gift". (The wording varies by region; either option leads to the gift flow.)
  5. In Amazon's gift form, paste the recipient's email (the one shown on this site — a regular email like name@example.com, not a @kindle.com address). Add an optional message and pick a delivery date.
  6. Click Place your order and pay as you would for any Amazon purchase.
  7. Return to this tab. When prompted "Did you complete the purchase?", click OK — the book is marked as gifted on the platform.

What the recipient experiences: Amazon emails them a redemption link. They click it, sign into their Amazon account, and the book lands in their Kindle library — readable on every Kindle device or app they use.

How the Platform Works

Simple & Transparent: People in need post Kindle book requests with their Amazon link and email address. Donors browse requests and purchase Kindle books on Amazon using the "Give as a Gift" option.

Instant Digital Delivery: No physical shipping. Amazon emails the recipient a redemption link, and the book appears in their Kindle library within minutes.

Your Privacy: Only approved requests are public, and we never store payment information — all purchases happen securely through Amazon.

Platform Support: This platform uses Amazon Affiliate links (tag: samuelkimanis-20). A small percentage of purchases helps cover hosting costs at no extra cost to you.

❓ Frequently Asked

How does Buy Me a Book work?
Readers post Kindle book requests with an Amazon link and their reason for wanting the book. Donors browse the requests, purchase Kindle books on Amazon using "Give as a Gift," and Amazon emails the recipient a redemption link. The book lands in the recipient's Kindle library within minutes.
Is Buy Me a Book free to use?
Yes. The platform is free for readers and donors alike. Donors pay only for the Kindle books they choose to purchase on Amazon. Buy Me a Book uses Amazon Affiliate links to help cover hosting costs at no extra cost to anyone.
How do I gift a Kindle book to someone?
Pick a request on the site and click Buy Kindle Book & Gift. A confirmation shows you the recipient's email and opens Amazon on the exact Kindle edition. On Amazon, click Buy for others or Give as a Gift, enter the recipient's email, and complete the purchase. Amazon emails them a redemption link they click to receive the book.
How do I request a Kindle book?
Open the Request a Book tab and fill out the embedded Google Form with the book's Amazon Kindle link, your email, and a short story of why you'd like to read it. Approved requests appear on the platform for donors to see.
Is my data safe?
Only approved book requests are public. Other personal data submitted via the form stays private. The platform never stores payment information — all purchases happen directly on Amazon under your own Amazon account.
Why don't I just buy the book for myself?
Many readers can't afford every book they want — students, people between jobs, readers in regions where prices are steep relative to local incomes. Buy Me a Book lets people who can spare a few dollars send a single book directly to someone who would value it deeply. It's a small kindness that lands instantly.
